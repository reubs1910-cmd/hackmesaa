FiscalFight

A TresDevs© game
🎓

Can you survive the semester?

You're a college student with bills, classes, and life coming at you. Make real decisions about money, and see if you can graduate with a Financial GPA of 4.0.

1. Pick a character — each has a different real-life financial starting point.

2. Survive 12 weeks. Each week you'll face a curveball.

3. Your choices shift your Cash, Credit, Stress, Debt, and Scholarship IQ.

4. At the end, you're scored on a 0.0 – 4.0 Financial GPA.

Financial GPA Breakdown:

4.0 - 3.7 (Dean's List 👑):

Elite money moves. You survived AND built wealth. Positive net worth, improved credit, found scholarships, avoided predatory debt.

3.6 - 3.0 (Honor Roll 🎓):

Strong semester. Solid financial decisions. Stayed afloat, managed stress, made smart aid choices.

2.9 - 2.0 (Passing 📚):

You made it through. Room to grow. Some debt accumulated, but you finished the semester.

1.9 - 0.1 (Academic Probation 😬):

Tight semester. Debt and stress piled up. Struggled with bills, missed opportunities.

0.0 (Dropped Out 💀):

You went BANKRUPT (cash below -$500)! Couldn't survive the semester financially.

Your GPA is calculated from:

• Survival (35%): Net worth at semester end

• Credit Growth (15%): Credit score improvement

• Savings Rate (20%): % of income saved

• Scholarship IQ (15%): Aid opportunities found

• Debt Avoided (15%): Predatory loans sidestepped
