Can you survive the semester?

You're a community college student with bills, classes, and life coming at you. Make real decisions about money, and see if you can graduate with a Financial GPA of 4.0.

1. Pick a character — each has a different real-life financial starting point.

2. Survive 12 weeks. Each week you'll face a curveball.

3. Your choices shift your Cash, Credit, Stress, Debt, and Scholarship IQ.

4. At the end, you're scored on a 0.0 – 4.0 Financial GPA.

Choose your character

Each character starts the semester with a different real financial situation.
