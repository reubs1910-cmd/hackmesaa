# FinPath - Hackathon Pitch

## One-Line Pitch
**An AI-powered personal finance platform for college students that matches them with scholarships, helps them manage money, and gives personalized financial guidance based on their real situation.**

---

## The Problem

**College students are drowning in financial confusion.**

- 70% of students report significant financial stress
- $4.5 billion in scholarships go unclaimed every year
- Most students don't understand their credit score or how to build it
- Financial aid is confusing, budgeting is hard, and there's no personalized help

**The gap:** Students need guidance tailored to their actual circumstances — not generic advice.

---

## The Solution

**FinPath: A personalized financial survival platform for college students.**

Users enter their profile once:
- School type (community college, state school, private)
- Age, income, dependency status
- GPA, major, year (freshman, transfer, etc.)
- Work status, basic financial info

Then FinPath gives them:
1. **Scholarships they're actually eligible for** — personalized matches based on their profile
2. **Budget breakdown** — realistic monthly spending plan for college life
3. **Financial aid gap estimate** — how much they need and where it can come from
4. **Credit improvement tips** — beginner-friendly education on building credit
5. **30-day action plan** — clear next steps to improve their financial situation

---

## Core Features (MVP)

### 1. Dashboard
- Personalized financial overview
- Gap estimate (tuition vs. aid vs. income)
- Next-step recommendations
- Action items for the next 30 days

### 2. Scholarships
- Smart matching based on:
  - GPA, major, school type
  - Income, dependency status
  - Transfer/freshman status
  - Background and demographics
  - Deadlines
- "Missed money" detector — shows unclaimed opportunities

### 3. Money Plan
- Simple monthly budget (rent, food, books, emergency fund)
- Spending guidance tailored to student life
- Financial aid gap calculator
- Savings recommendations

### 4. Credit Basics (Optional)
- Educational content on credit scores
- What affects credit as a student
- Beginner recommendations (secured cards, authorized user, etc.)

---

## What Makes It Stand Out

✅ **Practical** — solves a real, urgent problem  
✅ **Personalized** — not generic advice, tailored to each student  
✅ **Socially useful** — helps students avoid debt and find aid  
✅ **AI-powered** — chatbot for questions like "Can I afford to move out?" or "Should I take this loan?"  
✅ **Transfer-student focus** — underserved niche with unique needs  
✅ **Deadline tracker** — never miss a scholarship or FAFSA deadline

## Tech Stack (Suggested)

**Frontend:**
- HTML/CSS/JavaScript (current)
- React (if time allows)

**Backend:**
- Node.js + Express or Python + Flask
- Database: PostgreSQL or MongoDB

**AI/ML:**
- OpenAI API for chatbot
- Simple matching algorithm for scholarships (rule-based or ML)

**Data:**
- Scholarship APIs (e.g., Scholarships.com, College Board)
- Manual curated list for MVP
- FAFSA data (simulated for demo)

---

## Demo Flow

1. **User logs in** → enters profile (school, GPA, major, income, etc.)
2. **Dashboard loads** → shows financial gap, scholarships found, next steps
3. **Scholarships tab** → personalized list with deadlines and eligibility
4. **Money Plan tab** → budget breakdown, spending guidance, emergency fund advice
5. **AI chatbot** → user asks "Can I afford to move out?" → gets personalized answer

---

## Warnings / Disclaimers

- **Do not claim to give actual financial advice** — frame as "educational guidance"
- **Privacy matters** — if using real financial data, mention security/compliance
- **For hackathon, simulate data** — don't connect to real bank accounts or credit bureaus

---

## Final Recommendation

**Focus on 3 tabs:**
1. Dashboard
2. Scholarships
3. Money Plan

**Keep investments small or remove entirely.** Students need help with:
- Affording tuition
- Avoiding debt
- Budgeting
- Finding aid

Not stock picking.
